About Us

Our Story

[borrow] was created to connect people wanting to share their items, equipment or skills with those in their area looking to rent these goods.

In every household, countless items sit stagnantly in storage, waiting for you to find a use for them. Our platform allows you to list these items on a local board for people in your community to see. People can then request to rent your items for a flat daily fee. The customers rate your items, while you rate the customers. [borrow] is the first peer-to-peer rental platform allowing anyone to rent any of their posessions.

We hope to reduce the amount of time equipment sits idle by sharing it amongst the community; benefiting the community while allowing renters to make money.

[borrow] reduces the pressure for individuals to purchase material items for limited-time use.

Furthermore, we allow people to market their skills as freelance professionals. Oftentimes individuals require the assistance of someone with a specific skillset for only a short period of time (i.e. a DJ for a party, a photographer for a wedding). Borrow can also facilitate connections for these occasions.

Sharing is easy

Post items in less than 60 seconds and start earning money off of your unused goods.

  • Snap some pictures of your item
  • Mark our calendar for when you won't be using it
  • Set a per-day rent price

[borrow]ing is fun

[Borrow] items and equiptment that help you to pursue your dreams, for less.

  • Browse items and select the dates you would like to rent
  • Pay the listed price-per-day or bargain with the lender until an agreement is reached
  • Pickup item at a system-designeted location in your area

Your safety, our priority

  • Areas of exchange are mandated by [borrow] to be public, likely-populous areas
  • If an item is not as pictured or described when you arrive at the exchange, you will not be charged and [borrow] will freeze the account-in-question, pending review
  • Borrowers accepting items at pickup will be held liable, via our photo re-exchange process, for any damages incurred during their rental

Our Part

As facilitator of transactions, we take a miniscule portion of the profits (so we can keep [borrow] running).

Our fees are very simple and straightforward. For all [borrow] periods earning under $15 total, we take a flat commission of $2.95. You keep the rest. For [borrow] periods earning $15 or more, you keep 80% of your sale and our commission is 20%.
