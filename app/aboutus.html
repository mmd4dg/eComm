About Us

Our Story

[Borrow] was created to connect people wanting to share their items, equipment or skills with those in their area looking to [borrow] these goods.

In every US household, many types of tools, books, sporting equiptment etc. sit unused in storage. Prior to [borrow], a rental application consolidating the [borrow]ing of all local goods did not exist.

We hope to reduce the amount of time equipment sits idle by sharing it amongst the community; dually allowing individuals to profit off of their otherwise unused items.

[Borrow] reduces the pressure for individuals to purchase material items for limited-time use.

Furthermore, we allow people to market their skills as freelance professionals. Oftentimes individuals require the assistance of someone with a specific skillset for only a short period of time (i.e. a DJ for a party, a photographer for a wedding). Borrow can also facilitate connections for these occasions.

Sharing is easy

Post items in less than 60 seconds and start earning money off of your unused goods.

  • Snap some pictures of your item
  • Mark our calendar for when you won't be using it
  • Set a price per day for [borrow]ing

[borrow]ing is fun

[Borrow] items and equiptment that help you to pursue your dreams, for less.

  • Browse items and select the dates you would like to [borrow]
  • Pay the listed price-per-day or bargain with the lender, through our unique negotiating feature, until agreement
  • Pickup item at a system-designeted location in your area

Your safety, our priority

  • Areas of exchange are mandated by [borrow] to be public, likely-populous areas
  • If an item is not as pictured or described when you arrive at the exchange, you will not be charged and [borrow] will freeze the account-in-question, pending reviewal
  • [Borrow]ers accepting items at pickup will be held liable, via our photo re-exchange process, for any damages incurred during their rental

Our Part

As facilitator of transactions, we take a miniscule portion of the profits (so we can keep [borrow] running).

Our fees are very simple and straightforward. For all [borrow] periods earning under $15 total, we take a flat commission of $2.95. You keep the rest. For [borrow] periods earning $15 or more, you keep 80% of your sale and our commission is 20%.
