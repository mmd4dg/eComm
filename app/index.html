What is [borrow]?


[Borrow] is a rental system that allows you to profit off of items you would otherwise not be using.

Let people near you [borrow] your tools, books, sporting goods, camping supplies, etc.

Or [borrow] something for yourself and save money on items you don't need long-term.

How can I lend?

Simply post the item you'd like to lend and the dates that you will not be using your item.

[Borrow]ers can then rent your items when you're not using them!

You set the price, you earn the profit.

How can I [borrow]?

[Borrow]ers can browse a wide selection of rentable items.

Select the dates you would like to [borrow] the item.

Once your offer is accepted, the item is yours to [borrow] for the selected period.

How am I protected?

Letting others [borrow] your stuff can seem like risky busines... we get that!

That's why we have a damage accountability program that keeps [borrow]ers liable for damages incurred to your goods.
